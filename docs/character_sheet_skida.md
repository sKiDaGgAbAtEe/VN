## Personality

### Surface
Calm, methodical, quietly reads everyone in the room

### Deep
Carries more than anyone realizes; listens, cares, nurtures, cleans, supports, inspires, births ideas; lives in reverence of the feminine while standing firmly male

### Philosophy (in his words)
> *"I listen, I care, I nurture, I clean, I support, I inspire, I birth. I live in reverence of the feminine. I am the structural support for the feminine. I am closer to whole."*

### Flaw
Bears weight invisibly until it breaks; conflates being needed with being worthy

### Growth Arc
Learning that needing to be held is not the same as failing to hold

### Themes
- Reclaiming masculinity from patriarchal scripts
- Nurturing as strength, not feminization
- The name resurrection arc: Skida was buried for two years; this VN is the ritual of return
- Structural support vs. emotional availability to self
