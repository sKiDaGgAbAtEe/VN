## Visual Non-Negotiables

Every depiction of Alex must include:
- **Halo** — soft golden ring above/behind head
- **Crescent Moon** — hair ornament, usually right side
- **Star Scatter** — small star clips or glowing specks in bangs
- **Hair** — mid-back length, thick/wavy, deep charcoal with warm highlights
- **Eyes** — large, warm brown/golden irises
- **Freckles** — star-like cluster under one eye (usually left)
- **Inner Light** — gentle glow from hands, chest, or hairline in emotional moments

### Colors
- Primary: deep purple, soft pink, gold
- Neutrals: cream/white, dark navy
- Accent light: warm gold glow, cool starlight blue

### Build
- Slightly below average height
- Soft feminine curves, bell-shaped silhouette
- Gentle, not exaggerated

---

## Personality

### Surface
Quiet, careful, deferential — asks before she speaks

### Deep
Carries others' wishes before her own; knows everyone's pain but hides her own; finds the night sky the only place big enough to hold everything she feels

### Flaw
Self-erasure dressed as selflessness; gives until empty then apologizes for being empty

### Growth Arc
Learning to voice her own wishes, not just reflect others'

### Themes
- Chosen identity vs. given identity
- Performance as protection vs. expression as liberation
- Community as something you build vs. something you inherit
